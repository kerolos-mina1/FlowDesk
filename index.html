FAQ

Questions? We've got answers

How does the AI task assistant work?
Our AI is trained on millions of software projects and your own team's historical patterns. It learns your velocity, writing style, and workflow to generate relevant subtasks, flag risks, and suggest priorities. It gets smarter the more you use it — no configuration required.
Is my data private and secure?
Yes. FlowDesk is SOC2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Your data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We never train our AI on your private project data, and you can export or delete all data at any time.
Can I migrate from Jira, Linear, or Asana?
Absolutely. We offer one-click imports from Jira, Linear, Asana, Monday.com, Trello, and Notion. Your full task history, attachments, comments, and custom fields migrate automatically. Most teams are fully migrated in under 20 minutes.
How does the free trial work?
You get 14 days of full Pro access — no credit card required. At the end of the trial you choose a plan or drop to the free Starter tier. Nothing is deleted; your projects and data are always yours. No auto-charge surprises.
What happens if I exceed my plan limits?
We'll notify you before you hit limits, not after. On the Starter plan you'll get an upgrade prompt, but nothing stops working mid-sprint. On paid plans, you can enable auto-scale with a spending cap so you're never blocked and never surprised.
